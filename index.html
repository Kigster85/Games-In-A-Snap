About Games in a Snap

Game in a Snap is a fun filled, community driven, speed building event, which puts four participants up against one another, for two hours building off against one another. It is an event that is across multiple games, from Cities Skylines, Planet Coaster, Planet Zoo, and looking for more titles to bring in to the line up. Here are some of the top things why people love these events:

  • Friendly Competition where you can showcase your channel and building skills.
  • Community Run events, The staff who run the events do it out of their own time and love for the games.
  • Prizes for the participants, sometimes we get gifted cd-keys or merch and we feel the participants deserve to have these as prizes for taking part.
  • We enjoy having a laugh, creating memes, and enjoying watching each and every participants talents, helping channels grow and peoplem getting found more on twitch.

Staff

All of the staff here at Games in a Snap are from within the communities and do it out of the kindness of their hearts as each position is filled with a volunteer. If you would like to give us some of your spare time and help us out, check out the discord server for more information!
